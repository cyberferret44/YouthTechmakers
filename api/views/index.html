Our Mission

The world is on the eve of a technology Renaissance. To thrive in this future, individuals must be equipped with a variety of skills. Chief among those are the abilities to be adaptable, be curious and seek answers, communicate, empathize, self start, and challenge the status quo. Youth Techmakers is a Non-Profit entity which exists to empower youth to develop these skills while inspiring passion in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, and Digital Arts by providing resources and training to students, educators, and volunteers.
"I grew up in a small rust-belt town, but with a bit of luck found myself at Google as a Software Engineer. During a trip home I visited my former school to teach about Technology. I was humbled by the positive reception the students gave me and was inspired by their excitment to learn. It left me wanting to do more to empower them, and students like them, to discover and pursue their passions"
- Chase Violet

What we Offer

1. Makerspace Materials

Learning is enhanced when physical materials enable us to empirically experience the concept we're learning.

2. Learning Series

To provide instruction on how the Makerspace materials work and why.

3. Community Outreach

Social Capital is imperative for growth, so the final goal of Youth Techmakers is to help serve as a Community to connect students with professionals to help them better envision the possibilities available to them.
