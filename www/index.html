Youth Techmakers

Empowering students to explore Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, and Digital Arts through Makerspaces!

Our Mission

The world is on the eve of a technology Renaissance. To thrive in this future, individuals must be equipped with a variety of skills. Chief among those are the abilities to be adaptable, be curious and seek answers, communicate, empathize, self start, and challenge the status quo. Youth Techmakers exists to empower youth to develop these skills while inspiring passion in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, and Digital Arts by providing resources and training to students, educators, and volunteers.

Our Story

"I grew up in a rust-belt Ohio town, and one day found myself in the unlikely positon of Software Engineer at Google, something I would have never imagined in my youth. During a family trip home, I bumped into my 6th grade science teacher and ultimately returnd to teach the students about Engineering. I was overwhelmed and humbled by how well received I was, and I found the student's exciement to learn to be both infectious and inspiring. I left my home with a resolve to do more to empower those students, and more students like them, to discover and drive their passions." - Chase Violet

What we Offer

Learning is most effective when instruction meets student-driven exploration. Youth Techmakers exists to be a resource to promote STEAM (Science, Technology, Enginering, Art, and Mathematics) education by providing Materials and Guides that teach how to use them. While there are many great online resources, Youth Techmakers is unique in its commitment to incorporate materials that allow students to not only see, but also experience the concepts first-hand.
